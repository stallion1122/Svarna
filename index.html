Some words about SVARNA Training Institute

Successful training requires skilled practical trainers, who are capable of sharing their practical understanding in a unique environment, where every trainee and individual can grow & learn. SVARNA is committed to ensure that every trainee achieves as much as possible during their time with us, and we provide tangible enhancements to the professionals who enrol with us. We seek to move beyond the traditional method of didactic, ‘chalk and talk’ instruction to a modern system of technology-based pedagogical guidance, with high level of attention paid to all our trainees.

What we do?

At SVARNA we guarantee a well-rounded training and education. Our courses will help you develop analytical and problem solving capability, logical skills and practical experience along with leadership, business conceptualisation and presentation skills. Svarna provides customized training solutions for each client, based on their individual needs and unique characteristics that meet the specific needs of business, industry and commerce. We take the time to understand your organization before we start a training intervention. Our programs are then consistent & synergized with our client's standards & processes, applicable regulations, and current industry best practices. The training portfolio includes –

  • Individual & group language training
  • Secretarial and administrative training
  • Corporate Training
  • Behavioral & Soft Skills training
  • Leadership and Management Development training.

    Mission and Purpose

    The mission of SVARNA Training Institute is to facilitate student and employee personal development through quality training programs that meet the needs of the diverse community and population in the UAE:

  • To offer programs that focus on the applied concepts and skills required for success in a global economy.

  • To provide high quality curriculum developed and improved by faculty and administrators through regular evaluations and external consultation with industry.

  • To continually examine the evolving needs of students and employers to offer career oriented education programs.

  • To provide an interactive and collaborative educational environment that strengthens learning, provides credentialing opportunities and contributes to educational professional growth.

  • To provide students services that contributes to academic success, personal development and career progression.
